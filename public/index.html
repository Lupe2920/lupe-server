Tulafono · Terms & Conditions

Booking & Payment Terms

1. A 50% deposit is required at the time of booking. The remaining 50% is due 30 days before arrival. Bookings within 30 days require full payment upfront.

2. A refundable security bond is collected: $300 AUD (up to 5 guests) · $400 AUD (6–7 guests) · $500 AUD (8+ guests). The bond is always fully refunded regardless of cancellation, within 7 days of check-out or cancellation.

3. A 10% discount applies automatically to stays of 9+ nights.

4. Mid-stay clean: Complimentary for stays of 11+ nights, scheduled by mutual agreement.

5. Cancellation policy:

• 48-hour grace period — Full 100% refund if cancelled within 48 hours of booking, provided check-in is 45+ days away

• 90+ days before arrival — 85% cash refund · 15% non-refundable admin fee

• 60–89 days — 75% credit valid 12 months · no cash refund

• 30–59 days — 50% credit valid 12 months · no cash refund

• 14–29 days — 25% credit valid 12 months · no cash refund

• Within 14 days — No refund · No credit · Full loss

6. Credit conditions: Credits are subject to availability · Cannot be applied to peak season dates already heavily booked · Valid 12 months from issue date · Non-transferable · No cash value · If new dates cost more, guest pays the difference.

7. Car hire: Driver must be 25+ with a valid licence. Vehicle returned in same condition — damage costs are the guest's responsibility.

8. Babysitter: Available 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM. Minimum child age 12 months. Ages must be accurately declared at booking.

9. Check-in from 3:00 PM. Check-out by 11:00 AM. Late check-out or early check-in on request, subject to availability.
